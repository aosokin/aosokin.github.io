The TJPU-UOW Joint Research Center for Computer Vision is headed by Prof. Philip O. Ogunbona at University of Wollongong, Australia and Prof. Zhitao Xiao at Tianjin Polytechnic University, China. It is part of the Tianjin International Science & Technology Cooperation Center: Internalional Research Certer of Intelligent Information Processing Technology, which pursues the development and integration of innovative signal processing tools at various stages of the acquisition, analysis, and interpretation pipeline of imaging data. .

Research

I do research mostly in such fields as machine learning, discrete optimization and computer vision. Most of all, I like when knowledge accumulated in different fields comes together and resonates. My current projects are mostly concentrated around structured-output prediction: practical methods using deep learning and theoretical understanding of specific challenges of structured prediction.

About

The TJPU-UOW Joint Research Center for Computer Vision is headed by Prof. Philip O. Ogunbona at University of Wollongong, Australia and Prof. Zhitao Xiao at Tianjin Polytechnic University, China. It is part of the Tianjin International Science & Technology Cooperation Center: Internalional Research Certer of Intelligent Information Processing Technology, which pursues the development and integration of innovative signal processing tools at various stages of the acquisition, analysis, and interpretation pipeline of imaging data, in particular using bio-imaging and medical imaging techniques. Nowadays, we aim at obtaining new insights into eye-related Computer Aided Diagnosis system and multispectral analysis of biological signals.
